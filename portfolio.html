Favourite Destination:Manali

Manali is a beautiful hill station located in the state of Himachal Pradesh, India. Surrounded by snow-covered mountains and lush green forests, it is a popular destination for tourists throughout the year. The cool climate, scenic landscapes, and peaceful environment make it an ideal spot for relaxation and adventure. Attractions like Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass, and the Hadimba Devi Temple draw visitors from all over the country. Manali is also known for adventure sports like paragliding, skiing, and river rafting. Whether you're looking for natural beauty, spiritual calmness, or thrilling activities, Manali has something for everyone.
